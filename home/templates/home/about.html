About Vera & Her Craft

The story of Vera's Reclaimed Treasures began over 25 years ago in Malta, when Vera embraced her love for Art in creating painted gifts for friends and family. Rather than limiting herself to a standard canvas, Vera imbued her work with her passion for environmental causes and spiritualism, breathing new life into standard and oft-forgotten household items. Now commonly referred to as Upcycling, for Vera it is a process of surfacing, accentuating or enhancing the inherent value of these ordinary objects.

Over decades, Vera perfected her craft, progressing from creating solely for friends and family to hosting stalls at countless local Craft Fairs. Vera is now offering a curated selection of eco-conscious gifts in myriad styles through this site, along with the facility to get in touch for Made To Order, bespoke items.
